If you are looking to find a bike there are many things that you have to consider before you purchase this website will provide information for beginners who know nothing about bikes and people who are more experienced. Therefore the information that will be provided will be highlighting the different parts of a bike that can be viewed along with the sound of the bike.

The website will also include a small game that can be played to try and earn the highest score and A guide on what bikes are best for beginners and intermediate drivers including a description on the different types of bikes like adventure bikes.

Finally the website will include a page describing the best locations for certain types of bikes and A downloads page to get custom made wallpapers.
