France

When travelling through areas like france the adventure bikes are recommended due to the variety of terrain and the distance that will need to be travelled.


Staffordshire

When travelling through areas like Staffordshire or areas around it an adventure or off-road bike would be recommened as there are alot of country lanes that can be difficult to maneuver on other types of bikes.

Wales

To travel through areas like Wales A Sports Tourer and other types similar to it are recommended as there are alot of fast roads that appeal to the bike and what it can do.
